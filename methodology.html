Methodology

Process of Indian Justice System

  1. Filing of FIR or Complaint
  2. Investigation
  3. Charge Sheet Filing
  4. Framing of Charges
  5. Trial
  6. Judgment
  7. Appeal (if applicable)

This is a simplified overview. The actual process can be more complex depending on the nature of the case.
